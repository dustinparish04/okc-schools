District context

Public-school quality in OKC is a school-by-school story more than a district-reputation story — here's the backdrop before you get into individual schools below.

🧭 Edmond Public Schools

~25,900 students across 28 schools. Ranked top 10% of OK districts (37th of 532) on combined proficiency, with a 92%+ graduation rate at all three high schools. Niche ranks it #250 nationally. Oakdale (below) sits inside Edmond city limits but runs as its own single-school district.

🏙️ Oklahoma City Public Schools (OKCPS)

~33,200 students across 59 schools. District-wide proficiency is well below the state average, but that average hides a lot of range — magnet programs like Classen School of Advanced Studies and neighborhood schools like Wilson Elementary post scores well above the district norm.
